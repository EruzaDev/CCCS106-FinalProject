## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Login

The login page is the entry point to HonestBallot. Enter your credentials to access your dashboard.

**Steps:**
1. Enter your **Username**
2. Enter your **Password**
3. Click **Sign In**

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Security Note:** After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be locked for 15 minutes.

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (76).png>)

---

### 1.2 Sign Up

New voters can create an account through the sign-up page.

**Steps:**
1. Click **Create Account** on the login page
2. Enter your **Username**
3. Enter your **Email**
4. Enter a **Password** (must be 8+ characters with mixed case, digits, and special characters)
5. Click **Create Account**

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (77).png>)

## 2. Voter Guide

### 2.1 Voter Dashboard

After logging in as a voter, you'll see the main dashboard with candidate cards.

**Features:**
- View all registered candidates
- Search candidates by name, position, or party
- Compare candidates side-by-side
- Access the news feed

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (78).png>)

---

### 2.2 Viewing Candidates

Browse through candidate cards to learn about each politician.

**Each card shows:**
- Profile photo
- Name and position
- Political party
- Brief biography
- Verification badges

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (79).png>)
---

### 2.3 Comparing Candidates

Compare two candidates running for the same position.

**Steps:**
1. Click the **Compare** button on the first candidate
2. A banner appears showing compare mode is active
3. Click **Compare** on another candidate with the same position
4. View the side-by-side comparison

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (80).png>)

comparison mode

### 2.4 Viewing Candidate Profile

Get detailed information about a candidate.

**Steps:**
1. Click **View Profile** on any candidate card
2. View their full biography, achievements, and records

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (79).png>)

### 2.5 Casting Your Vote

When COMELEC starts a voting session, you'll be redirected to the voting page.

**Steps:**
1. Select one candidate for each position
2. Review your selections
3. Click **Submit Vote**
4. Confirm your vote

> ‚úÖ **Note:** You can only vote once per election session.

## 5. NBI Officer Guide

### 5.1 NBI Dashboard

NBI officers manage legal records for candidates.

**Dashboard includes:**
- Record statistics
- Add new records
- Search and filter records
- Manage record status

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (94).png>)

---

### 5.2 Adding Legal Records

Add legal records for politicians.

**Steps:**
1. Click **Add Record**
2. Select the politician
3. Choose the record type (Graft, Corruption, Criminal, etc.)
4. Enter title and description
5. Select the date
6. Click **Submit**

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (95).png>)

---

### 5.3 Managing Records

Update or verify existing records.

**Steps:**
1. Search for a politician
2. View their records
3. Click **Verify** or **Edit** as needed
4. Update status (Pending, Verified, Dismissed, Rejected)

![alt text](<screenshots/Screenshot (96).png>)
